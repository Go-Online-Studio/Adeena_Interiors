Residential Interior Design

A complete home design path for clients who want layout clarity, elegant finishes and a coordinated execution experience.

Overview

A home should feel tailored before it feels decorated.

Residential interior design at Adeena Interiors starts with the practical realities of the home: storage, circulation, lighting, maintenance, family routines and hosting patterns. Once the floor plan works, the studio develops a visual language through material palettes, custom furniture, lighting layers and decor styling.

The result is a space with polish and restraint: premium enough to feel special, grounded enough to support daily life.
